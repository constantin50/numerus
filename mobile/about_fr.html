“Number rules the universe.”

― Pythagoras

One of the hardest parts of learning a foreign language is listening, especially numbers (15100, 0.931, 1201, 9001210, etc.). But a clear understanding of them is crucial in economics, physics, geography, history and other sciences that deal with numbers.

The goal of this web site is to provide intensive training of comprehension of numbers pronounced in different contexts: news, lecture, tv shows, blog, etc.

So, choose a language, listen to a video, write the number that you hear, press a check button, see subtitles. Then, go to the next video.

Several others languages are planned to add. If you speak language that is not here and want to help contact me.
